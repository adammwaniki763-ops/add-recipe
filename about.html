About Recipe Keeper

Our Mission

Recipe Keeper is dedicated to helping home cooks digitize and organize their favorite recipes in one beautiful, easy-to-use place. We believe that recipes are more than just instructions—they're stories, memories, and culinary traditions worth preserving.

Why We Built This

Traditional recipe cards and cookbooks are wonderful, but they're not searchable, shareable, or accessible from every device. We created Recipe Keeper to bridge that gap: a personal recipe management app that combines the warmth of a handwritten cookbook with the convenience of modern technology.

Features

  • Save Your Recipes: Add unlimited recipes with ingredients, instructions, and images
  • Organize by Category: Tag recipes to keep them organized
  • Quick Access: View all your recipes in a beautiful grid
  • Local Storage: Your recipes stay on your device—no accounts needed

Design Philosophy

We designed Recipe Keeper with a modern editorial aesthetic inspired by beautiful cookbooks. Warm colors, elegant typography, and generous whitespace make cooking feel like a creative, personal ritual. Every element is intentional and curated, just like flipping through a beautifully designed cookbook.
